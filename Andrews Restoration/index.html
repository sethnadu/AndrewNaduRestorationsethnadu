A before and after of a renovated family room/playroom in our own renovation project! This room was one of the 2 kitchens in our home, and the location directly off of our new kitchen seemed like the perfect place to put our family room. We had to completely gut what was there but once done we had such a great space to work with! We chose to do a shiplap throughout with varying sized boards, built a large closet for the kids toys/crafts, and made a beautiful wooden radiator cover. Lots of natural light and neutral colors with all the wood makes this room warm and inviting!
